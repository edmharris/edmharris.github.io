Travel

Africa

Ngorongoro Crater, Tanzania

Before moving to Canada, I travelled to Africa, taking in Kenya, Tanzania, Zanzibar and South Africa. One of the highlights was embarking on a safari in the Serengeti National Park, including the incredible sights and wildlife in the Ngorongoro Crater.

Picture courtesy of Mariola Grobelska on Unsplash

China

Terracotta Warriors, Xi'An

In the same year, I travelled to China, starting in Hong Kong, taking in Shanghi, Hangzhou, Bejing, Xi'An and Kunming, before crossing the boarder into Laos. One of the highlights was visiting the terracotta warriors in Xi'An, an epic site that makes you think about the wonders of the ancient world.

Picture courtesy of Aaron Greenwood on Unsplash

India

Taj Mahal, Agra

While there were many other sights in between, the final leg of my travels took me to India, starting in New Delhi and visiting Agra, Jaipur, Jodhpur, Varanasi and Kolkata. One of the most stiking sights was the Taj Mahal, both in its grandeur and mystery.
